Overview:

Business travelers need to find ways to relax during their downtime while on the road. Traditional travel apps don't adequately address the unique needs of business travelers looking for quick activities. The ShowMe Travel mobile travel application is designed to meet the pain points of business travelers by providing access to quick, local events and activities that can be explored during downtime between meetings and appointments.
